pin          =  just a number from 1 to 12
color        =  cable color (visible on img/display.png)
arduino pin  =  arduino pin of course :)
display pin  =  number: a ground of display `x`, letter(s): segment `x`
+/-          =  + if it is a positive pin (connected to a positive end of the LED inside the display), else -
